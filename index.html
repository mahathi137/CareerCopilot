Frequently Asked Questions

Is CareerCopilot completely free?
+

Yes! CareerCopilot is completely free to use. All features including DSA practice, aptitude tests, interview prep, and the AI Copilot are available at no cost.

How does the Placement Readiness Score work?
+

Your Placement Readiness Score is calculated based on your progress across all modules: DSA completion (30%), Aptitude accuracy (20%), Interview prep (20%), Resume score (20%), and Consistency streak (10%).

Is my progress saved if I close the browser?
+

Yes! All your progress, completed questions, bookmarks, notes, and settings are saved in your browser's Local Storage. Your data persists across sessions on the same device and browser.

Can I use CareerCopilot on mobile?
+

Absolutely! CareerCopilot is fully responsive and works seamlessly on mobile devices. Practice on the go with the same great experience.
